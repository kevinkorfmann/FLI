==========================================
Part V: Advanced and Research Topics
==========================================

This part of the guide ventures beyond the classical likelihood toolkit and into
the broader landscape of modern statistical inference.  We begin with the deep
connections between likelihood and Bayesian reasoning, move through the
computational machinery that makes Bayesian and high-dimensional inference
feasible, survey the principled methods for choosing among competing models, tour
the research frontiers where likelihood ideas meet machine learning, and close
with the numerical craftsmanship that underpins every reliable implementation.

Each chapter is self-contained but builds on the foundations laid in Parts I--IV.
Cross-references point back to earlier material whenever a concept is reused.
